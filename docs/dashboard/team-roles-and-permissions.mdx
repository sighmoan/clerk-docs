---
title: Team Roles and Permissions
description: Learn about the different roles available for team members on a Clerk Dashboard.
---

Clerk Dashboard offers various roles to manage team members access. Different roles ensure that team members have the appropriate level of access according to their responsibilities.

### Admin Role

Admins have full access and control over all team and applications settings.

### Member Role

Viewers can view content but cannot make changes.

### Developer Role

Developers integrate Clerk's authentication, manage user and organizations data.

### Support Role

Support has access to impersonate users, and ban users, with less autonomy to
integrate Clerk's authentication.
